- **Genuine Intentionality** - Self-generated goals from internal state evaluation
- **Operational Meta-Cognition** - Differential bias detection across cognitive nodes
- **Behavioral Self-Correction** - Avoids failing pathways without external instruction

### What's Honestly Reported
- Self-awareness scored 0.0 because the conflict threshold wasn't met (pathway strength 0.053 < 0.5 required)
- Creativity scored 0.2 due to missing domain knowledge, not architectural limitation
- The 0.0 and 0.2 scores are reported alongside the 1.0 and 0.7 scores
